# Product Recommendation Assistant Case Study

## Objective

Design an AI assistant that recommends products based on customer needs, category, budget, and preferences.

## Business Problem

Customers may not know which product is suitable for their needs. Manual consultation can take time, especially when a shop has many similar products.

## Proposed Assistant Flow

1. Ask the customer what type of product they need.
2. Ask about budget.
3. Ask about product category or personal preference.
4. Filter suitable options.
5. Return a short recommendation with reason.

## Example Input Factors

- Product category
- Budget range
- Skin type or product need
- Customer preference
- Frequently asked questions

## Example Conversation

**User:** I need a product for oily skin under 300k.  
**Assistant:** Sure. Do you prefer cleanser, toner, serum, or moisturizer? I can recommend a suitable option based on your budget and skin type.

## Deliverables

- Product recommendation prompt
- Product filtering logic
- Sample chatbot responses
- API response format
- Basic frontend chatbot UI flow

## Evidence Value

This case study demonstrates chatbot use case analysis, recommendation workflow planning, and prompt engineering for business automation.
