# Internal Business FAQ Assistant Case Study

## Objective

Create a chatbot concept that helps internal staff quickly find answers from business policies, onboarding notes, and internal FAQ documents.

## Business Problem

Internal staff may need to search policy notes, workflow rules, or onboarding documents. Manual search can be slow when documents are scattered.

## Proposed Solution

The assistant uses a structured FAQ/knowledge base and prompt templates to answer internal business questions. A future version can use retrieval-augmented generation with document chunks.

## Supported Use Cases

- Staff onboarding FAQ
- Policy questions
- Workflow explanations
- Internal knowledge search
- Simple document-based Q&A prototype

## Deliverables

- Internal FAQ knowledge base format
- Prompt engineering templates
- Sample question-answer pairs
- Deployment guide
- User guide for internal staff

## Evidence Value

This case study demonstrates understanding of knowledge assistant workflows and business process automation.
